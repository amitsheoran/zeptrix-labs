[ 02 ] What It's Like Here

Who thrives at Zeptrix.

We're upfront about who fits. Saves everyone time.

  • → 01

    Senior mentorship, always

    Whether you're 11 years in or just starting out, you sit next to people who've shipped real things. Code reviews, architecture chats, career advice - built into the week.

  • → 02

    Real ownership, not theatre

    We don't have product managers writing tickets for engineers to silently complete. You talk to clients, propose solutions, and own outcomes. Junior or senior - it's the same expectation, with the right support.

  • → 03

    Remote-first, not remote-only

    Work from anywhere. Drop into the Pilani office whenever you want. Quarterly team meetups for everyone, on us. We measure trust, not hours.

  • → 04

    We say no to bad work

    If a project would force us to ship something we'd be embarrassed by, we don't take it. That's the boring secret to staying proud of what you do.
