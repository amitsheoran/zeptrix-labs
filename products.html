ReviewBoost

QR-powered Google review engine
Live · v1.0

ReviewBoost is a QR-code-powered SaaS that helps local businesses - restaurants, cafes, salons, gyms, clinics, shops - collect more Google Maps reviews by generating natural, AI-suggested reviews their customers can copy with one tap. Print one QR per location, scan it on a phone, pick a few things the customer enjoyed, and ReviewBoost drafts four review variants in English, Hindi, Hinglish, or mixed - tuned to the customer's rating. Low ratings get quietly routed to a private feedback inbox instead of going public.

DigiMenu

QR menus & WhatsApp ordering
Coming Soon

DigiMenu is a digital menu platform for hotels, restaurants, and cafes - replacing printed menus with a single scannable QR code on every table. Guests browse the menu on their phone, build an order, and submit it directly to the kitchen or counter through WhatsApp. No app installs, no waiting for a server, no reprinting menus when prices change. Just one QR, always up to date.

Category
B2B SaaS · Hospitality
Stack
Next.js · Supabase · WhatsApp Cloud API
Channel
QR-only · No app install
Status
In design - early 2026
  • One QR per table - guest scans and the live menu loads instantly
  • Order routed straight to the kitchen or counter via WhatsApp
  • Menu editor with categories, photos, prices, and availability toggles
  • Daily specials, combos, and discount tags without reprinting menus
  • Multi-language menu support for tourist-heavy locations
  • Lightweight - no app install, opens in any phone's browser
  • Analytics on most-viewed and most-ordered items
  • Hotel-friendly: room-service mode with room-number routing
